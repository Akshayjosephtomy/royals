ABOUT US..

OUR BRAND PURPOSE

At Rajasthan Royals, we are on a mission to “Transform Society through Cricket, and Cricket through Innovation”. We are a global sports franchise that believes that royalty is not something one inherits, it is something that is to be earned. This 'Spirit of Royalty' is what sets Rajasthan Royals apart from the myriad other sports franchises globally. We call it being Royal at Heart. So what exactly does it take to be a Royal at Heart? Winning in style. Never losing without a fight. Being resilient. Standing up – for yourself, the team and the game. Setting the bar high and leaving a legacy behind. But being large-hearted and welcoming at the same time. All heart. All guts. That's what makes a Royal. It is this spirit that has led Rajasthan Royals to discover, nurture and promote new talent the future stars of Indian cricket. The Royals are also known for their innovative and pioneering approach.

A CLUB BUILT ON STRENGTH, COURAGE AND FIGHTING PROWESS

The Royals brand stands for respect, pride and an undying spirit. With roots in the culturally rich state of Rajasthan, the Royals are impassioned by indomitable qualities of strength, courage and fighting prowess which personify the brand. The franchise is born from the commitment to entertain cricket fans and unearth new talent across the globe, and predominantly in India. Since our inception, we have been at the forefront in terms of nurturing and promoting new talent, making us one of the most loved IPL teams in India and globally.
