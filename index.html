OUR BRAND PURPOSE

At Rajasthan Royals, we are on a mission to “Transform Society through Cricket, and Cricket through Innovation”. We are a global sports franchise that believes that royalty is not something one inherits, it is something that is to be earned. This 'Spirit of Royalty' is what sets Rajasthan Royals apart from the myriad other sports franchises globally. We call it being Royal at Heart. So what exactly does it take to be a Royal at Heart? Winning in style. Never losing without a fight. Being resilient. Standing up – for yourself, the team and the game. Setting the bar high and leaving a legacy behind. But being large-hearted and welcoming at the same time. All heart. All guts. That's what makes a Royal.

THE ANTHEM - HALLA BOL

“Halla Bol” started off as a battle cry for the team and has been the anthem of the Rajasthan Royals ever since. The Royals’ brand of cricket has received praise for its spirit, aggression and the ability to display courage in the face of adversity. The phrase “Halla Bol” represents the same intensity and fighting spirit that is synonymous not just with the team, but also with the people of Rajasthan.

ROYALITY

Royalty and team spirit form the core values of the Royals’ logo that has been designed to evoke pride and grandeur. The lion buglers celebrate the large RR symbol that occupies the central area of the logo. While the Rajasthan Royals are all about being competitive and fair on the field, the logo epitomizes revelry, respect and pride. The logo was redesigned in 2019 after valuable feedback from our fans on elements that needed strengthening.
